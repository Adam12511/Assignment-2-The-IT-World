Collective Comparison

Each member of the team has chosen jobs that have been categorised as high skill level, require an analytical mindset and demand a tertiary education qualification (which RMIT will hopefully provide us) according to Labour Market Insights . Unsurprisingly, the common general skills between our jobs are excellent communication and teamwork, as none of the positions would be isolated to solitary work a majority of the time, such as cybersecurity specialists who would need a team to both investigate and develop security systems for their organisations, or a neuroscientist on a university sponsored research project with assistants and colleagues of the same field. Database organisation and programming are also common among the demand between these jobs, indicative of the market in the information age.
