Autonomous Vehicles

The results show that most are expecting the first generation of autonomous vehicles to be released commercially around 2024 and later more complex autonomous vehicles such as trucks to come within the following decade. Countries are currently assessing autonomous vehicles with some countries allowing for the use of autonomous vehicles on roads and others in the process of being passed for use on roads, which can be seen in figure 1. With time autonomous vehicles will be seen commonly on the roads as the technology continues to rapidly improve and the market allows for easier purchasing of autonomous vehicles and more common modes of transport becoming autonomous. Further developments in machine learning and 3D imaging technology make autonomous vehicles possible to be made as without either of these technologies autonomous vehicles would not be able to be produced. Improvements in that such as better 3D tracking via camera and improved AI and machine learning will allow for better autonomous vehicles with lower risks and allow them to be able to be brought to a wider market with some countries that may be against autonomous vehicles also adopting them into their country.
