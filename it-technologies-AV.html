Autonomous Vehicles

An autonomous vehicle allows for machine learning and 3D live imaging to be able to drive itself from one point to another point without needing a human to interact with it. Autonomous vehicles are an up-and-coming technology which are currently in development and are becoming increasingly better over the years as current technology improves and will make autonomous vehicles a widely used technology in the future. A state of the art autonomous vehicle currently has LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging), RADAR (Radio detecting and ranging) and a camera to help with 3D imaging, which all help to pinpoint the vehicle’s location and help it navigate through its surroundings. Autonomous vehicles also have state of the art machine learning involved which has been refined over many years so that it can be used in autonomous vehicles and be able to learn pathways to help in directing autonomous vehicles and to be able to conduct the autonomous vehicle in a safe manner so that no accident is caused. It is also a state of the art car with all the features that any current car being developed might have, such as reduced energy consumption. Further developments in LiDAR, RADAR and camera tracking technology will allow for more precision in autonomous vehicles and can help stop accidents from happening as well as help solve the issues they are currently facing with weather conditions such as fog and rain which interrupts the 3D imaging and makes it harder on autonomous vehicles to navigate under these weather conditions. There will also be improvements in the software used in these autonomous vehicles so that no issues will be found with them that may cause accidents or for an error to occur that is not meant to happen in the autonomous vehicle such as inputting a destination and being taken to the wrong destination. These are current issues which autonomous vehicle manufacturers are trying to solve right now for the future improvement of autonomous vehicles with some of the problems on track to being fixed within the next decade. Within the next couple of years some companies manufacturing autonomous vehicles are planning to release them to the commercial market for use on roads for all and will be available for everyone to buy. A survey was conducted by McKinsey&Company in which 75 executives from companies such as automotive, transportation, and software that are working on autonomous vehicles and autonomous driving worldwide were asked about when autonomous vehicles would start to be rolled out with the results being in figure 1.

The results show that most are expecting the first generation of autonomous vehicles to be released commercially around 2024 and later more complex autonomous vehicles such as trucks to come within the following decade. Countries are currently assessing autonomous vehicles with some countries allowing for the use of autonomous vehicles on roads and others in the process of being passed for use on roads, which can be seen in figure 1. With time autonomous vehicles will be seen commonly on the roads as the technology continues to rapidly improve and the market allows for easier purchasing of autonomous vehicles and more common modes of transport becoming autonomous. Further developments in machine learning and 3D imaging technology make autonomous vehicles possible to be made as without either of these technologies autonomous vehicles would not be able to be produced. Improvements in that such as better 3D tracking via camera and improved AI and machine learning will allow for better autonomous vehicles with lower risks and allow them to be able to be brought to a wider market with some countries that may be against autonomous vehicles also adopting them into their country.

Impact of Autonomous Vehicles

The impact that autonomous vehicles will have will cause a massive change in the vehicles industry as it is a brand-new technology for the market. It is likely going to make more people start to buy autonomous vehicles over the standard non-autonomous vehicle due to it being simpler and more convenient for the driver not having to drive, allowing them to not have to interfere with the car in any way. Autonomous vehicles will also affect everyone in the future as autonomous vehicles become more common and will change the way people travel from one destination to another destination as they become commonplace on forms of public transport and personal vehicles. Autonomous vehicles will replace some jobs such as ones related to driving like, truck drivers that deliver supplies, taxi drivers or train drivers and more as these will be operated by autonomous vehicles which don’t need human input to drive them. Autonomous vehicles will not only replace but also create jobs as well in fields such as software developing, AI and 3D imaging which are needed for the continual production and improvement of autonomous vehicles. Companies such as Ford, one of the leading car manufacturers behind autonomous vehicles have put out job requests for jobs such as AI investment and other companies such as GM another leading car manufacturer behind autonomous vehicles have also advertised for new jobs under autonomous vehicles. The manufacturing of autonomous vehicles has overall lead to the creation of a whole new job field which has already give thousands of jobs to people and will only increase as autonomous vehicles continue to be demanded and once released on the market will only increase even higher in demand which will lead to more jobs in the field in the future.

Effect of Autonomous Vehicles

Autonomous vehicles will change my daily life by changing the ways that I travel as autonomous vehicles will become more and more common in public transport. It may make me start using them more in my daily life due to the convenience that they provide over a non-autonomous vehicle. Others will also be affected in their daily life by using autonomous vehicles when doing things such as a daily commute to work and may choose to use autonomous public transport which is open 24 hours or private autonomous vehicles more often as it becomes more convenient and less intensive due to not having to drive and can just wait in the vehicles while they go from one destination to another. Through the use of autonomous vehicles, the time it takes travelling between places will also go through a change as more people adopt the using of autonomous vehicles over non-autonomous vehicles to help create a safer road through using autonomous vehicles over non-autonomous vehicles as they will be safer due to the human error that can happen in a non-autonomous vehicle. Autonomous vehicles will allow for more places to be more easily connected as they will be able to travel great distances without the need to stop and can make long distance travel much easier for everyone and can allow things such as food, parcels and more to be sent more quickly than before. Autonomous vehicles will help in preventing supply issues which may be caused in the past other than natural disasters and allow for cheaper prices on goods. Long-distance travel will also be easier allowing for more people to be able to spread out and to be able to visit other far away locations more easily, helping solve issues with isolated communities, with issues such as a lack of medical resources.
